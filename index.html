About

She built what didn't exist.

Beyond The Buyout™ was founded to prevent institutional intelligence from going dark. Following the 2025 federal workforce shifts and widespread buyouts, one structural reality became clear: there was no disciplined infrastructure to convert federal expertise into independent, procurement-ready capacity. There was resume guidance. There was retirement guidance. There was no architecture for continuity. Beyond The Buyout™ was built to close that gap.

Institutional expertise deserves continuity — not dormancy.

Carmen S. Artis

MBA  |  MSc Contracts & Procurement  |  AI Consultant Certified

Founder, Beyond The Buyout™  |  Compound Growth Holdings Co.

Carmen spent 17 years inside HHS agencies navigating procurement systems, multimillion-dollar oversight, and high-accountability public service governance. In March 2025 — one year ago — she made a strategic departure following major workforce restructuring, balancing family responsibilities alongside a career she had given everything to. She identified a systemic gap no one had filled. Beyond The Buyout™ is her response.
